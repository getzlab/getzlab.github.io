Yotam Drier is a Principal Investigator at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, where he and his team study epigenomics of cancer and other diseases. Their research integrates epigenetic characterization, comprehensive big data analysis, experimental perturbations and validations, and machine learning based algorithms.
